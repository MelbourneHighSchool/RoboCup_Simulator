Medium Motor¶

class ev3sim.devices.motor.ev3.MediumMotor(*args, **kwargs)¶
off()¶

Turns the motors off indefinitely, until further calls are made.

on(speed, **kwargs)¶

Turn the motors on indefinitely at a certain speed.

Parameters

speed (float) – Any number from -100 to 100. Negative values turn the motors the opposite direction.

on_for_degrees(speed, degrees, **kwargs)¶

Turn the motors on for a certain amount of degrees. Note that further calls to motors may interrupt this behaviour.

Parameters

  • speed (float) – Any number from -100 to 100. Negative values turn the motors the opposite direction.

  • degrees (float) – Any number, amount of degrees to rotate, Negative values will turn the motors the opposite direction.

on_for_rotations(speed, rotations, **kwargs)¶

Turn the motors on for a certain amount of rotations. Note that further calls to motors may interrupt this behaviour.

Parameters

  • speed (float) – Any number from -100 to 100. Negative values turn the motors the opposite direction.

  • rotations (float) – Any number, amount of rotations to complete, Negative values will turn the motors the opposite direction.

on_for_seconds(speed, seconds, **kwargs)¶

Turn the motors on for a certain amount of time. Note that further calls to motors may interrupt this behaviour.

Parameters

  • speed (float) – Any number from -100 to 100. Negative values turn the motors the opposite direction.

  • seconds (float) – Any positive number. Seconds the motors will stay this speed for.
